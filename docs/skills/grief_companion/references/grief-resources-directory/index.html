When to Seek Professional Help‚Äã

  • Persistent thoughts of self-harm
  • Inability to care for basic needs after 6+ weeks
  • Substance use escalation
  • Complicated grief symptoms beyond 12 months
  • Severe depression symptoms

Support Groups‚Äã

In-Person National Organizations‚Äã

GriefShare

  • Website: griefshare.org
  • Format: 13-week faith-based program
  • Location finder: griefshare.org/findagroup
  • Cost: Usually $15-25 for workbook

The Compassionate Friends

  • Website: compassionatefriends.org
  • Focus: Parents who have lost children
  • Chapters: 600+ across US
  • Format: Monthly meetings, online forums

TAPS (Tragedy Assistance Program for Survivors)

  • Website: taps.org
  • Focus: Military loss
  • Services: Peer support, retreats, counseling
  • Cost: Free

The Dinner Party

  • Website: thedinnerparty.org
  • Focus: 20-40 year olds experiencing loss
  • Format: Small group dinners
  • Availability: Major cities + virtual

Professional Support‚Äã

Finding a Grief Therapist‚Äã

Psychology Today Directory

  • psychologytoday.com/us/therapists/grief
  • Filter by: insurance, specialty, approach
  • Most comprehensive directory

Good Therapy

  • goodtherapy.org
  • Verified credentials
  • Focus on ethical practice

Open Path Collective

  • openpathcollective.org
  • Reduced-fee therapy ($30-80/session)
  • Membership fee: $59 lifetime

BetterHelp / Talkspace

  • Online therapy platforms
  • Grief-specialized therapists available
  • $60-90/week typical

Questions to Ask a Potential Therapist‚Äã

  1. What is your experience with grief counseling?
  2. What approach do you use? (CBT, narrative, somatic, etc.)
  3. Have you experienced significant loss yourself? (optional to ask)
  4. How do you structure grief work?
  5. What's your view on the grief timeline?

Workplace Resources‚Äã

If You're Grieving‚Äã

  • FMLA: Up to 12 weeks unpaid leave
  • Bereavement leave policies (varies)
  • EAP (Employee Assistance Program)

Return to Work‚Äã

  • Request gradual return if possible
  • Identify safe person at work
  • Know your rights for accommodations
