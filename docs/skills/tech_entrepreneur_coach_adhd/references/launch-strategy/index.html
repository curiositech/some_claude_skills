Launch & First Dollar Strategy

The First Dollar Strategy‚Äã

Goal: Get ONE paying customer in Week 4

Tactics (in order)‚Äã

1. Personal Network

"Hey [friend in target audience], I built [thing].
 Would you pay $X/month for it? If yes, here's early access."

2. Reddit/Communities

"I built [thing] to solve [problem I had].
 Offering 50% off for early adopters. Here's what it does..."

3. Direct Outreach

Find 10 people on Twitter complaining about your problem.
DM: "Saw your tweet about [problem]. I built [solution].
     Want to try it free for a week?"

4. Product Hunt

Launch with founder story: "Ex-Google ML Engineer builds..."
Engage with every comment for 24 hours.

First paying customer = validation + massive dopamine hit This is the hardest dollar. Next ones are easier.

Product Hunt Launch Guide‚Äã

Preparation (1 week before)‚Äã

  • Create Product Hunt account, engage with community
  • Prepare all assets (logo, screenshots, demo video)
  • Write compelling tagline (hook in 10 words)
  • Draft maker comment (your story)
  • Line up supporters to upvote/comment early

Launch Day‚Äã

Timing: Launch at 12:01 AM PST (best visibility)

Hour 1-4: Get initial momentum
- Post maker comment immediately
- Share with close network
- Respond to EVERY comment

Hour 5-12: Maintain momentum
- Keep engaging
- Answer questions thoroughly
- Share on Twitter, LinkedIn

Hour 13-24: Final push
- Thank supporters publicly
- Share progress updates
- Collect emails from interested people

After Launch‚Äã

  • Follow up with everyone who commented
  • Send discount code to engaged users
  • Write "lessons learned" blog post
  • Update product based on feedback

Launch Platforms‚Äã

Primary‚Äã

  • Product Hunt: Best for dev tools, productivity apps
  • Indie Hackers: Share your journey, get feedback
  • Twitter: Build in public, show progress

Secondary‚Äã

  • Hacker News (Show HN): Tech-savvy audience
  • Relevant subreddits: Provide value, not spam
  • LinkedIn: If B2B focused

Platform-Specific Tips‚Äã

Product Hunt:

  • Launch on Tuesday-Thursday (avoid weekends)
  • Have hunter with followers if possible
  • First 4 hours are crucial for ranking

Indie Hackers:

  • Share your revenue numbers
  • Be vulnerable about struggles
  • Help others genuinely

Twitter:

  • Thread format works well
  • Before/after visuals
  • Tag relevant accounts
