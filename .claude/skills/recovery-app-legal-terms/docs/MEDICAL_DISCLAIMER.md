## Full Medical Disclaimer

**IMPORTANT: PLEASE READ THIS DISCLAIMER CAREFULLY**

### Not Medical Advice

Junkie Buds 4 Life ("the App") provides peer support, educational resources, and community features for people in recovery. **This App is NOT a medical service.** The information, tools, and community discussions provided through this App are for informational and peer support purposes only.

This App is not intended to:
- Diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or condition
- Replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment
- Serve as a substitute for the advice of a qualified healthcare provider
- Provide emergency or crisis intervention services

### Always Consult Professionals

You should always seek the advice of your physician, psychiatrist, therapist, or other qualified healthcare provider with any questions you may have regarding:
- A medical or mental health condition
- Medication changes or management
- Treatment options
- Starting or stopping any medication, including medication-assisted treatment (MAT)

**Never disregard professional medical advice or delay seeking it because of something you read or experienced in this App.**

### Emergency Services

**This App is NOT equipped to handle medical emergencies.**

If you are experiencing a medical emergency, please:
- **Call 911** or your local emergency number immediately
- Go to your nearest emergency room
- Contact your local poison control center if applicable

If you are in crisis or having thoughts of self-harm:
- **Call or text 988** (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline)
- **Text HOME to 741741** (Crisis Text Line)
- **Call 1-800-662-4357** (SAMHSA National Helpline)

### Peer Support Limitations

The community features of this App connect you with other people in recovery. While peer support can be valuable:

- Other users are **not licensed healthcare professionals** (unless explicitly stated)
- Advice from other users should not replace professional guidance
- What works for one person may not work for another
- No one in this community can prescribe medication or provide medical treatment

### No Guarantees

Recovery is a personal journey, and outcomes vary for each individual. We cannot and do not guarantee:
- Any specific recovery outcomes
- That you will achieve or maintain sobriety
- That the App will meet your specific needs
- That community advice will be accurate or helpful for your situation

### Your Responsibility

By using this App, you acknowledge that:
- You are responsible for your own recovery decisions
- You will consult qualified professionals for medical decisions
- You will not rely solely on this App for crisis support
- You understand the limitations of peer support
