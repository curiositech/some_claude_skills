Our community is built on mutual support and respect. You agree to follow our [Community Standards](link-to-community-standards), which include:

### Required Behavior

- Treat others with respect
- Share from your own experience ("I" statements)
- Respect privacy (what's shared here stays here)

### Prohibited Behavior

- **Personal attacks** - Attacking people, not ideas
