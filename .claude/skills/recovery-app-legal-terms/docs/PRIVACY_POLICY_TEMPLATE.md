## Our Privacy Promise

Your recovery journey is personal. We believe your data should be too.

**What's yours is yours.** We will never sell your personal data. Your journal entries, check-ins, and private reflections belong to you.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

| Data Type | Examples | Purpose |
|-----------|----------|---------|
| **Account Information** | Email, display name, avatar | Account creation, identification |
| **Profile Information** | Sobriety date, recovery pathway, handle | Personalization, community features |
| **Journal & Check-ins** | Daily reflections, mood tracking, HALT checks | Personal recovery tracking |
| **Community Content** | Forum posts, comments, reactions | Community support features |
| **Saved Meetings** | Favorited meetings, location preferences | Meeting finder convenience |

### Information Collected Automatically

| Data Type | Examples | Purpose |
|-----------|----------|---------|
| **Usage Data** | Pages visited, features used, session duration | Improving the app |
| **Device Information** | Device type, operating system, browser | Technical compatibility |
| **Log Data** | IP address, timestamps, error logs | Security, debugging |

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- **Real names** (unless you choose to share them)
- **Health insurance information**
- **Financial information** (we don't sell anything)
- **Location tracking** (location is only used when you search for meetings)
- **Biometric data**

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

| Purpose | Legal Basis |
|---------|-------------|
| Provide the App and its features | Contract performance |
| Maintain your account | Contract performance |
| Enable community features | Legitimate interest |
| Improve the App | Legitimate interest |

## 3. How We Share Your Information

### Publicly Visible (Your Choice)

- Display name (chosen by you)
- Avatar (chosen by you)
- Forum posts and comments
- Reactions to community content

### Never Shared

- Email address (only visible to you)
- Journal entries
- AI journal conversations
- Check-in data
- Saved meetings

### Third-Party Services

We use limited third-party services to run the App:

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Supabase | Database, authentication | Account data, app data |
| Cloudflare | Hosting, security | Technical data |
| Anthropic | AI features (optional) | AI conversation context |

All third-party providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.

### Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, such as:
- Valid court orders
- Law enforcement requests with proper legal process
- Protecting safety (imminent threat of harm)

We will notify you of such requests unless prohibited by law.

---

## 4. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Account data | Until you delete your account |
| Journal entries | Until you delete them |
| Forum posts | Until you delete them (or 30 days after account deletion) |
| Usage analytics | 2 years (anonymized) |
| Security logs | 90 days |

### After Account Deletion

When you delete your account:
- Personal data is deleted within 30 days
- Forum posts are anonymized (author shown as "Deleted User")
- Backups are purged within 90 days
- Some anonymized usage data may be retained for analytics

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

| Right | How to Exercise |
|-------|-----------------|
| **Access your data** | Download your data from Settings |
| **Correct your data** | Edit your profile anytime |
| **Delete your data** | Delete account from Settings |
| **Export your data** | Download option in Settings |
| **Opt out of analytics** | Toggle in Privacy Settings |

### California Residents (CCPA)

You have additional rights under CCPA:
- Right to know what data is collected
- Right to delete your data
- Right to opt out of data "sales" (we don't sell data)
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

### EU/UK Residents (GDPR)

You have additional rights under GDPR:
- Right to data portability
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to object to processing
- Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

## 6. Security

We protect your data with:

- **Encryption in transit** (TLS 1.3)
- **Encryption at rest** (AES-256 for sensitive data)
- **Access controls** (principle of least privilege)
- **Regular security audits**
- **Secure authentication** (Supabase Auth)

### Security Incident Response

If we discover a data breach affecting your personal information:
- We will notify you within 72 hours
- We will explain what happened and what data was affected
- We will describe steps we're taking to address it
- We will provide guidance on protecting yourself

- Standard Contractual Clauses (EU)

- Contractual protections with all processors
