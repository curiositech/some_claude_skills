What Makes a Good Skill?

  • Specific: Targets a particular domain or task type
  • Actionable: Provides clear guidance Claude can follow
  • Bounded: Knows what it's NOT for (avoids scope creep)
  • Unique: Doesn't duplicate existing skills
  • Tested: You've tried the approach and it works

Examples of Good Skills

  • cv-creator - Specific (resumes), bounded (not cover letters), has anti-patterns
  • drone-cv-expert - Domain expertise, pairs with inspection specialist
  • diagramming-expert - Clear activation triggers, output format guidance
